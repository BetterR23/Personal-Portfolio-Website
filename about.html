About me

Front-end developer from Dar es Salaam, Tanzania

I never thought I'd end up as a web developer. I felt lost and unsure of what I wanted to do with my career life. I decided to take a break from university to figure that out. I tried a few different jobs, but nothing really clicked for me.

One day, I stumbled upon a YouTube tutorial on web development and decided to give it a try. To my surprise, I found that I loved it. There was something about the process of building a website from scratch that really spoke to me, I was determined to make it work and decided to join SheCodes workshop after a-lot of research. Now I am a certified web developer and have landed my first internship as a software developer at Ennovate for Change. Imay havenot followed a traditional path to get here, but I am grateful and excited to for my new career path.

I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I have experience building responsive websites using Bootstrap. I'm also comfortable using GitHub for version control and Netlify for deploying and hosting web applications. I'm passionate about creating high-quality, well-designed websites, and I'm always eager to learn new skills and technologies.

I'm a perfectionist who loves a challenge and is always eager to learn. My ultimate goal is to become a front-end developer known for delivering high-quality work. I'm interested in advancing to a role as a software engineer and am committed to studying different courses to help me reach my goal.
